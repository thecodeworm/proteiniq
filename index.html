Project Description

This project, ProteinIQ, aims to analyze and calculate protein content in both animal-based and plant-based foods, addressing a gap in current protein analysis methods. With the rising popularity of plant-based diets and the increasing need for sustainable food sources, it's crucial to provide detailed information on alternative protein options. This experiment directly compares protein content from both animal and plant sources to inform dietary planning and optimize protein intake. Through this analysis, individuals can make informed decisions about their food choices, benefiting those transitioning to plant-based diets, athletes seeking alternative protein sources, and healthcare professionals advising patients. Understanding protein intake through daily foods is essential for balanced nutrition and achieving individual health goals.
